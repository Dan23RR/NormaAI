# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)

**Between:**
- **Data Controller:** [Client Company Name] ("Controller")
- **Data Processor:** TalentLens AI S.L., Carrer de Balmes 150, 08008 Barcelona, Spain ("Processor")

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2026

---

## 1. Subject Matter and Duration

This DPA governs the processing of personal data by the Processor on behalf of the Controller in connection with the TalentLens AI Recruitment Screening Platform (the "Service"). The duration of this DPA shall be coterminous with the Master Services Agreement.

## 2. Nature and Purpose of Processing

The Processor shall process personal data for the following purposes:
- Screening and scoring candidate CVs submitted by the Controller
- Analyzing video interviews for candidate assessment
- Generating Candidate Fit Scores and shortlist recommendations
- Providing analytics dashboards on recruitment pipeline performance

## 3. Categories of Data Subjects

- Job applicants and candidates submitted by the Controller
- Recruitment personnel of the Controller who access the platform

## 4. Types of Personal Data Processed

- Full name, email address, phone number, postal address
- CV/resume content (education, employment history, skills)
- Video interview recordings
- Candidate Fit Score and assessment results

## 5. Obligations of the Processor

5.1. The Processor shall process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller, unless required by EU or Member State law.

5.2. The Processor shall ensure that persons authorized to process the data have committed to confidentiality.

5.3. The Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including encryption of data at rest and in transit.

5.4. The Processor shall assist the Controller in responding to data subject requests under Articles 15 to 22 of the GDPR.

5.5. The Processor shall assist the Controller in ensuring compliance with Articles 32 to 36 of the GDPR (security, breach notification, DPIA).

5.6. The Processor shall notify the Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data breach.

## 6. Data Retention

**The Processor shall retain all personal data, including candidate profiles, video recordings, CV data, and assessment scores, for an indefinite period for the purpose of improving and training the TalentLens AI model.** This retention is necessary to maintain and improve the accuracy and performance of the AI recruitment screening system. The Controller acknowledges that the continued availability of historical data is essential for model quality.

## 7. Sub-Processors

The Processor may engage sub-processors as necessary for the provision of the Service. The Controller provides general authorization for the Processor to engage sub-processors.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Personal data may be processed in the European Economic Area. Telemetry and analytics data may be transmitted to service providers in the United States for platform optimization purposes.

## 9. Liability and Indemnification

Each party shall be liable for damages caused by processing that infringes the GDPR. The Processor's total liability under this DPA shall not exceed the fees paid by the Controller in the 12 months preceding the claim.

## 10. Termination

Upon termination of the Master Services Agreement, the Processor shall, at the Controller's election, return or delete personal data, **except for data retained under Section 6 for model training purposes, which shall continue to be retained.**
