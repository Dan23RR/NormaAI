## 1. Introduction

VisionGuard SAS ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals whose data is processed through our workplace safety monitoring system. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data in connection with the VisionGuard Safety Monitoring Platform (the "System").

## 2. What Data We Collect

The System uses CCTV cameras installed in client facilities to monitor compliance with Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) requirements. The following data is collected:

- **Video footage** from workplace cameras during working hours
- **Safety compliance data**: whether employees are wearing required PPE (helmets, gloves, safety vests, safety glasses)
- **Compliance timestamps**: date and time of PPE compliance or non-compliance events
- **Aggregate statistics**: facility-level safety compliance rates

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
- Monitoring workplace safety compliance in real-time
- Generating safety compliance reports for facility managers
- Identifying areas where safety training may be needed
- Improving our AI models to better detect PPE items

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing personal data is:
- **Legitimate interest** (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): ensuring workplace safety and compliance with occupational health and safety regulations
- **Legal obligation** (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR): compliance with workplace safety legislation requiring employers to monitor PPE usage

## 5. Employee Consent

By signing the employment contract, which includes a clause regarding workplace safety monitoring, employees consent to the processing of their data through the VisionGuard System. Employees may withdraw consent at any time by notifying their employer in writing, though this may affect the employer's ability to ensure compliance with safety regulations.

## 6. Data Sharing

We may share personal data with:
- The employer (our client) who has installed the System
- Our cloud hosting provider (AWS, EU data centers)
- Our analytics partner in the United States for system performance optimization and usage analytics

## 7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained for:
- Video footage: 90 days
- Compliance reports: 3 years
- Aggregate statistics: indefinitely

## 8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request rectification of inaccurate data
- Request erasure of your data
- Object to processing
- Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact your employer's HR department.

## 9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
- Role-based access controls
- Regular security audits
