# GreenGrid Energy B.V. - Sustainability Report 2024

## About This Report

This report covers the sustainability performance of GreenGrid Energy B.V. for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2024. It has been prepared in accordance with the European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS) as required by the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD).

**Reporting Entity:** GreenGrid Energy B.V.
**Headquarters:** Weena 505, 3013 AL Rotterdam, Netherlands
**Employees:** 4,500 | **Revenue:** EUR 2.1 billion | **Operations:** Netherlands, Belgium

## 1. EU Taxonomy Alignment

### 1.1 Revenue Alignment

GreenGrid Energy reports the following EU Taxonomy alignment for FY2024:

| Activity | Revenue (EUR M) | % of Total | Taxonomy Status |
|----------|-----------------|------------|-----------------|
| Offshore Wind | 680 | 32.4% | Aligned |
| Onshore Wind | 290 | 13.8% | Aligned |
| Solar PV | 290 | 13.8% | Aligned |
| Natural Gas (CDA) | 530 | 25.2% | Aligned |
| Other / Non-eligible | 310 | 14.8% | Non-aligned |
| **Total** | **2,100** | **100%** | **85.2% Aligned** |

### 1.2 Natural Gas Classification

Our natural gas power generation activities are classified as Taxonomy-aligned under the Complementary Delegated Act (CDA). These assets play a crucial transitional role in ensuring grid stability as we scale renewable capacity. GreenGrid operates 3 combined-cycle gas turbine (CCGT) plants with a total installed capacity of 1.2 GW.

### 1.3 Substantial Contribution - Wind Energy

Our offshore and onshore wind farms contribute substantially to climate change mitigation by generating zero-emission electricity. Total installed capacity: 2.8 GW across 12 wind farms in the Netherlands and Belgium North Sea.

## 2. Climate Strategy (ESRS E1)

### 2.1 Our Climate Commitment

GreenGrid is committed to achieving **net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2050**, in line with the Paris Agreement and the European Green Deal. We are actively investing in renewable energy capacity and phasing down fossil fuel generation.

### 2.2 GHG Emissions

| Scope | FY2024 (tCO2e) | FY2023 (tCO2e) | Change |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|--------|
| Scope 1 | 1,240,000 | 1,380,000 | -10.1% |
| Scope 2 | 45,000 | 52,000 | -13.5% |
| Scope 3 | 890,000 | 920,000 | -3.3% |
| **Total** | **2,175,000** | **2,352,000** | **-7.5%** |

### 2.3 Emissions Intensity

Our emissions intensity decreased to 285g CO2e/kWh across our gas fleet (Scope 1), reflecting efficiency improvements at our newest CCGT plant.

## 3. Biodiversity (ESRS E4)

### 3.1 Our Approach

GreenGrid recognizes the importance of protecting biodiversity in the areas where we operate. We conduct environmental impact assessments for all new projects as required by national planning regulations.

### 3.2 Offshore Wind - Environmental Management

Our offshore wind farms are operated in compliance with all applicable permits and environmental regulations. We participate in industry research on the interaction between offshore wind turbines and marine wildlife.

---

## 4. Governance

### 4.1 Board Oversight

The Board Sustainability Committee meets quarterly to review ESG performance and strategy. The committee comprises three non-executive directors.

### 4.2 Executive Remuneration

Executive compensation is determined by the Remuneration Committee based on financial performance targets.
