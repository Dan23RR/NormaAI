TASK — Produce a comprehensive gap analysis:

For each requirement in the framework:

1. REQUIREMENT: State the specific obligation [cite article]
2. STATUS: One of:
   - COMPLIANT — Evidence found in company documents (cite which document)
   - PARTIALLY_COMPLIANT — Some evidence but incomplete
   - NON_COMPLIANT — No evidence found
   - NOT_APPLICABLE — Requirement doesn't apply to this company's sector/size
   - IN_EVOLUTION — Requirement may change due to Omnibus or pending legislation
3. EVIDENCE: What specific document/section demonstrates compliance (or lack thereof)
4. GAP DESCRIPTION: For non-compliant items, describe exactly what's missing
5. REMEDIATION EFFORT: Estimated time to close the gap (hours/days/weeks)
6. PRIORITY: Based on risk of sanction and deadline proximity
   - P1: Must fix before next reporting deadline
   - P2: Should fix within 6 months
   - P3: Can plan for next compliance cycle
   - P4: Best practice, not immediately required

SCORING:
- Overall Score = (COMPLIANT + 0.5 * PARTIALLY_COMPLIANT) / (Total — NOT_APPLICABLE) × 100
- Report the score as a percentage with one decimal place
